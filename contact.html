Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our platform and courses.

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply browse our course catalog, select the course you're interested in, and click "Enroll Now." You can start learning immediately after payment confirmation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. We also offer installment plans for courses over $100.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your course, contact our support team for a full refund.

Do you offer certificates upon completion?

Absolutely! All our courses provide completion certificates that you can add to your resume and LinkedIn profile.

How long do I have access to my courses?

You have lifetime access to all courses you purchase. You can revisit the content anytime and access updates and new materials.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes! Our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices, allowing you to learn on the go with offline capabilities.
